Vehicle Sales Performance Rewards Bonus Enrollment

Enrolling your Sales Consultants in CPO Performance Bonus

If dealership is eligible for CPO Perfomance Bonus, enrollment information will display below the notes.

Please review the following notes:

  • The Sales Consultant Retail Performance Bonus Program has been moved to SFE.
  • Enrollment in the CPO Performance Bonus Program(s) is at the Dealership level for eligible Cadillac CPO and CPO dealers and includes all Sales Consultants in the Performance Recognition program.
  • CPO Sales Consultants who are enrolled by the dealer in the Bonus component, have met the minimum sales qualifier below, and all other qualifiers set forth in these rules, will earn a Bonus payout of $50 per eligible VIN sold and delivered (by the same Sales Consultant) during the program period.
  • The CPO Performance Bonus Program is paid once each year - in the first quarter following the end of the program year.
  • Dealership contributions to the Performance Bonus Programs will be held until the annual payments are made.
  • CPO Bonus payments will be credited to the Dealer's Open Account during the first quarter of 2016. Dealers will receive an individual Sales Consultant report which outlines their sales and earnings for the 2016 program.
  • If a Sales Consultant is unenrolled in the Performance Rewards Bonus Program during the year, the Dealer will receive the unenrollment credit to the Dealer Open Account in the quarter following the unenrollment.
  • Sales will be tracked separately for each Bonus Program in which a Sales Consultant is enrolled. If the Sales Consultant is enrolled in more than one Bonus Program and has met the minimum sales to qualify for each Program (and has met the other program qualifiers) then the Sales Consultant is eligible for payout in each Program.
