2017 Mark of Excellence

Master Certified Technology Expert

New for 2016!

The Mark of Excellence program now offers a Certified Technology Expert the opportunity to become a Master Certified Technology Expert and earn program awards.

Enrollment in the Master CTE program is completely optional, dealers not wanting to participant should elect not to enroll any participants on the enrollment roster.

To be eligible for enrollment in the MOE Master CTE Performance Recognition program, Certified Technology Experts:

  • Must have CTE as one of their roles in Center of Learning
  • Must be 100% Center of Learning CTE certified by Quarter 4, 2015
  • Must have dealership scorecard metrics of 70% DMN and 30% RemoteLink usage as measured by the January 15, 2016 file (vehicle sales data for October, November, December 2015)

note: Dealers may select "Enrolled" for only one CTE for enrollment into the MOE Master CTE Performance Recognition program.

Certified Technology Experts will be declared Mark of Excellence Master Certified Technology Expert Performance Recognition winners if they meet or exceed:

  • Successful completion of the Hands-On In-Vehicle Assessment, and
  • Lead 3 quarterly customer clinics, and
  • Center of Learning CTE Certification qualifier (includes all CTE courses), and
  • Dealer Maintenance Notification (DMN) year-end Target of 80% (defined as the January 15, 2017 file including vehicle sales data for October, November, December 2016), and
  • RemoteLink Mobile App Usage Target of 40% (defined as the January 15, 2017 file including vehicle sales data for October, November, December 2016)

Certified Technology Experts enrolled in the Master Certified Technology Expert program will be informed via email regarding their enrollment and the steps needed to complete Master Certification.
