Please review the following notes:

  • Performance Recognition and Performance Bonus Programs have changed for the following job types.
    • Parts/Body Shop Manager, Consultants and Other Service personnel
    • Service Managers and Consultants
    • Service Technicians
  • Enrollment in the Service Technician Performance Bonus program requires an additional $30 per Service Technician per month enrollment fee.
  • ealers are responsible during this enrollment period to determine the amount of earnPOWER points that will be paid to qualifying participants in each of the Performance Bonus programs in which they choose to enroll (Performance Bonus is 100% dealer-funded).

  • Enrollment in the Fixed Operations Performance Bonus Program(s) is at the Dealership level and includes all Fixed Operations personnel enrolled in the Performance Recognition program.
  • The Performance Recognition and Performance Bonus payout takes place at year-end for all job types.
