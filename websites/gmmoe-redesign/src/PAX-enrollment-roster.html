New! Master Certified Technology Candidates

This section is prepopulated with CTEs who may be eligible for the Master CTE program. Final eligibility is based on successful participation in the Master CTE pilot and those personnel who:

  • Complete all applicable 2015 Center of Learning CTE Certifcation requirements
  • Meet or exceed the OnStar Dealer Maintenance Notification (DMN) activation rate of 80%
  • Meet or exceed the OnStar RemoteLink Mobile App usage of 40% as measured by the 2015 Q4 3 month rolling average

These are the only personnel allowed to participate in the Master CTE program.
