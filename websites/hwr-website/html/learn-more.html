Hardware Rewards - A loyalty rewards program for the independent Hardware Retailer


Hardware Rewards is a free points program that rewards independent hardware retailers for their ongoing brand loyalty. It’s a way for leading brand manufacturers to thank you, the independent store, for carrying their products in your store. Members earn points on each purchase and the points can be redeemed toward exclusive rewards.

  • Hardware Rewards for Participants
+  Who is the program for?
Hardware Rewards is a rewards program for independent hardware stores. The program is currently limited to the United States.
+  What are the benefits of the program?
Hardware Rewards lets you earn rewards for your business, your employees and yourself. You earn reward points just for buying quality products from leading brands in your industry. It’s completely free and easy to participate. Your membership in Hardware Rewards will also give you access to:

• New product introductions
• Business-building ideas and resources
• Networking with other independent hardware store professionals
• Member only exclusive promotions
• And more…
+  How do I earn points?
It’s easy!

  1. Enroll for free at www.hardwarerewards.com

  2. Purchase brands from sponsoring manufacturers – there are lots of them!

  3. Submit a picture of your invoice using the Hardware Rewards App and points will automatically be deposited into your account
+  How to I redeem my points for rewards?
You simply redeem points on the program website. There is an online catalog with over 350,000 rewards to choose from including electronics, jewelry, tools, books, travel gear, experiences, and sporting goods, from quality brands like Bose, Apple, Tiffany and Company, Sony, Panasonic, Kitchen Aide, Schwinn to name a few.
+  How does this program help me with my business?
Redeem your points for reward items that you can use to motivate, recognize and retain your valuable employees, at no cost to you. Employees that are motivated by tangible rewards will strive to increase productivity, up-sell your customers, comply with safety standard and make sure you customers are satisfied.

Hardware Rewards can help to keep your customers happy too. Use your points to get a new plasma TV, coffee machine or popcorn machine to your store location to make your customers visit more pleasant.

The program website will also allow you to the access to other free business-building ideas and to connect to other independent hardware store professionals.
+  What’s the catch?
There is no catch. . It is simply a way for leading brand manufacturers to thank you, the independent hardware store, for supporting their products. It’s completely free to join and to participate. Your rewards add up fast because there are so many eligible products in Hardware Rewards, and the list of eligible products will continue to grow over time.

Start earning points for what you are already doing - buying quality products from quality brands.
